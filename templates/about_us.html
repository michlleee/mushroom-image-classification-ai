Hello There!

Welcome to SafeShroom, an AI-powered tool designed to help you safely identify mushrooms in nature. Our website allows users to upload pictures of mushrooms, which are then analyzed using advanced image recognition technology to determine if they are edible or poisonous. With the rise in foraging and outdoor activities, we wanted to create a reliable and easy-to-use platform that empowers everyone—from seasoned mushroom hunters to curious beginners—to stay safe while exploring the natural world. Our AI model has been trained on thousands of mushroom images to provide accurate results, giving you peace of mind during your next outdoor adventure.

SafeShroom was created by three university students passionate about combining technology with nature to make a positive impact. We saw the dangers posed by poisonous mushrooms and wanted to leverage our knowledge in AI and machine learning to create a solution that helps prevent mushroom-related poisoning. Our goal is to provide an accessible tool that educates and protects people in a fun, user-friendly way. Whether you're foraging for food or simply fascinated by fungi, SafeShroom is here to ensure you can enjoy your foraging journey safely.
